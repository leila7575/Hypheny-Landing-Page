Hypheny
Your SECURE link to REAL TIME CONNECTIONS

Welcome to Hypheny! Your messaging app that allows you to chat with your friends, family and colleagues with complete peace of mind. Hypheny prioritize your privacy by refraining from collecting data for commercial use. Our clean, user-friendly interface allows you to connect without distractions, keeping the focus on meaningful interactions. Enjoy a simple, intuitive and safe messaging experience.

Features

Secure Conversations

With Hypheny, you can safely communicate, knowing that every message you sent is encrypted and protected. Safeguard your private conversations with the people you trust.

Personalized Contact Options

Take control of your contact list and choose who to connect with. With the personalized contact list management, you can send friendship requests, accept new friends or block contacts.

Customizable Message Backgrounds

Avoid confusions and misunderstandings. As it's sometimes difficult to grasp the intention behind a message, we give you the option to indicate with each sent message the background color that best represents your emotion.

About This Project

Hypheny was inspired by the need of a private messaging experience. While most messaging apps collect large amounts of personal information for advertising and analytics purposes, we prioritize the user's privacy with no data collection for marketing or analytics purposes.

Then, experiencing various misunderstandings and misinterpretations during oral or written conversations, and particularly during written messages, gave us the idea of incorporating an element of non-verbal communication into the messages. This gave birth to the idea of offering customizable backgrounds to represent the intention behing every message sent.

This project was developed as our portfolio project, as part of our first year of training in foundations of computer science and softwer development.
